A Budget Friendly Outing for Friends and Family at Bantayan, Cordova.

Bantayan Bay at Bantayan, Cordova, is a budget friendly outing place for friends and family. You can have your stay at the Sandbar or along the mangroves for a shade.

Bantayan is the perfect go-to to unwind and swim your stress away without worrying too much about your budget.

Though there's no place that provide a good shade, aside from the mangroves, you can bring a tent or a tarp to place at the sandbar for a temporary shade.

Whats more is that you can enjoy a instagrammable sunset painted sky when the sun is setting.

And if you have the extra budget for a grill, you can do so just at the entrance of Bantayan Bay.
