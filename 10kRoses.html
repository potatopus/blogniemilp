A Beautiful Place to Relax: 10k Roses



If you're looking for a place to relax near a coast, then 10k Roses is the place for you. A cafe that is in a perfect spot for relaxation and planespotting.

10000 Roses is a cafe with it's main attraction the White Roses that lights up in the night, located at the end of Cordova Tourism Center, just right next to Lantaw Floating Restaurant.

My expections are high because of it's popularity that's been a talk since it's opening in 2017. Though my expectations were not met, it is still indeed a beautiful sight. I like the atmosphere there, it is relaxing. There's also a lounge area where you can sit or lie down to chill and watch the magnificient view.

If you're considering getting here, I would recommend to do so early evening just in time for sunset painted sky, if the weather is good, and the glowing of the roses for a much more instagrammable photos.
