A Walk Through Cordova Centennial Road / Cordova-Roro Port Rd

The Cordova-Roro Port Rd is a place that's good for a walk or jog. This leads to a food court where there are a lot of food choices to choose from different stalls.

The best time to walk through this road is early in the morning or late afternoon to capture the sunset or maybe for your date. In here you can have your daily walks, jogs, or just stroll to have some air and take a break from school or work.

At the entrance of the road you will be greeted with food stalls where you can buy streetfoods, Isaw, Fried Manok, and boiled corn on the cob to name a few. The stalls usually open up in late afternoon, usually around 5 to 6. But worry not if you're an early bird person, there are still some foods that you can buy and consume, like Lugaw and Puto og Sikwati, to start your day.

But wait, there's more! Along the shore there are plenty of food and beverages that you can choose from different stalls for a night out with family or friends. And as for me, it is the Tuslob Buwa. Other dishes that you may find include: Barbeques, Milkshakes or Milkteas, classic Filipino Foods and many more.
