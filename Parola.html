Parola Seaview Restaurant: Authentic Filipino Foods

The Parola Seaview Restaurant is located at Roro Port Cordova. A restaurant that offers authentic filipino foods and good beverages.

The foods here are delicious and are not too expensive. And with their nice location you can enjoy a stunning and panoramic view of the sea from sunrise to sunset.

I recommend that you dine here and have your early dinner to experience and enjoy your day as you are looking towards the beautiful sunset.
