Cordova Museum: Glimpse of Cordova's History



Get a glimpse of the history of Cordova. Cordova Museum is located at Bantayan Rd, Cordova, Cebu. It is home to some of Cordova's historical collections.

If you want to know about Codova's history, Cordova Museum gives you the oppurtunity to get a hold of how Cordova came to be and how its corresponding barangay's name came to as what it is.

Though as important as it may sound the museum be, holding and preserving Cordova's history, it could get some improvement. I just can't help but feel like it is neglected.

But it is still an honor to be able to get to know more about Cordova. I just wish there was more to see.
