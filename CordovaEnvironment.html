A Cleaner and Greener Cordova



Cordova is home to thousands of people and littering has made the enrvironment, which is vital to our livelihood, deteriorate making it hard for the fishermen in securing food.

The enrvironment we live in is a gift from nature and where we are dependent on. But as we continue to live forward, what we did to it in return is by littering.

This begs the question, "In what way are we to continue on living in harmony with the enrvironment?" Well, the simple answer to that is by being responsible and simply taking care of it.

Let us make Cordova have a Cleaner and a Greener Environment. We, BSIT 2D, did our part by cleaning up the coastal area of Centennial Road.

This made the environment cleaner but without the cooperation of the many, the Cordovahanons, the people, this action will be in vain.

Let us be responsible and dispose of garbage properly. Let us not litter. Lets preserve the environment. Do your part by starting at your home.
